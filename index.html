Biography

Sundar Pichai, born in Chennai, India, in 1972, is a renowned technology executive. He joined Google in 2004, contributing to the development of Chrome and Android. As CEO since 2015, Pichai led Google's transformation into Alphabet's subsidiary, overseeing its flagship products and innovations in AI, cloud computing, and more. He holds degrees from IIT Kharagpur and Stanford University, and his leadership has reinforced Google's position as a global tech powerhouse. Pichai's achievements have solidified his status as a prominent figure in the tech industry.
